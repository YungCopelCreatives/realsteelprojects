About Our Company

Real Steel Projects is a well-established contracting company that has provided turnkey building solutions to public and private sector clients across the country since 2015 with an exceptional bouquet of services that is founded upon values such as honesty, reliability, and quality. We have earned a reputation as a trusted small to medium enterprise contractor for construction, maintenance, plumbing, and shop fitting projects of all sizes. From new structures—commercial and residential—to the restoration of buildings, we are in our element on the building site, regardless of the nature or requirements of a project.

Mission

To build structures that are solid and timeless.

Vision

To be a world-class medium-sized construction entity in the Southern African market.

Goals

  • Complete all projects timeously
  • Excellent workmanship
  • Attention to detail
